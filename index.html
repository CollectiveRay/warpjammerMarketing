WarpJammer

The year is 2265. With the population of Earth swelling beyond what the planet can sustain, small expeditionary spacecrafts, known as "Jammers", are dispatched to find habitable planets via warp tunnels to nearby galaxies. Each warp brings the chance for glory… or untimely demise in the cold expanse of space.
