WarpJammer

A game for Apple Watch

The year is 2265. With the population of Earth swelling beyond what the planet can sustain, small expeditionary spacecrafts, known as "Jammers", are dispatched to find habitable planets via warp tunnels to nearby galaxies. Each warp brings the chance for glory…or untimely demise in the cold expanse of space.

Play Anytime

Forget your iPhone! Listen to an 8-bit soundtrack and sound effects play on your phone as you zoom through space all on your Apple Watch.

Gameplay

Use the Digital Crown to dodge missiles, alien ship fire, and anything else space may bring. Collect from 4 different powerups - shield your ship, gain extra time, and more

Gamecenter

Earn 20+ unique achievements as you play. Compete with your friends, beat their scores, and prove you’re the baddest of this generation of Jammers.
